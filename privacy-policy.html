Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we manage your personal data when you participate in 1x fantasy cricket tournaments. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information while you enjoy our fantasy cricket platform.

Information Collection

We collect data to enhance your experience and improve our fantasy cricket tournaments. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, contact details, and account information necessary for participating in 1x fantasy cricket tournaments.
  • Game Data: Details about your selected fantasy teams, tournament participation, player statistics, and performance history.
  • Usage Data: Information on your interactions with our platform, including IP addresses, browser details, and site engagement.

Use of Information

Your data is used to:

  • Manage and enhance your fantasy cricket experience.
  • Optimize game mechanics, scoring systems, and player selections.
  • Provide customer support and tournament updates.
  • Improve our platform based on user behavior and feedback.
  • Ensure fair play and prevent fraudulent activities.

Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information during transmission and storage.
  • Regular security audits to identify and prevent threats.
  • Limited access to personal data for authorized personnel only.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share necessary information with trusted partners to enhance the 1x fantasy cricket tournament experience, including:

  • Game analytics providers to refine tournament structures.
  • Service providers for data processing and customer support.
  • Technology platforms to improve the game environment and user experience.

All partners must adhere to strict data protection standards and only use your data as outlined in this policy.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our platform uses cookies and tracking technologies to improve your experience. Cookies store preferences and help us analyze game performance. You can manage your cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect some platform features.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and request a copy of the information we store.
  • Update or correct inaccurate details in your account.
  • Request deletion of your data, except where required for game integrity or tournament history.

To exercise these rights, contact us through our support channels. We will respond as soon as possible.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to support your participation in 1x fantasy cricket tournaments and improve our services. Data related to gameplay, scoring, and transactions may be kept to ensure fair play and tournament accuracy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our fantasy cricket tournaments. Any updates will be posted on our platform, and we encourage you to review them regularly.

Children's Privacy

Our fantasy cricket tournaments are intended for adult participants. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, we will take immediate action to remove it.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We are committed to providing a secure and enjoyable fantasy cricket experience.
